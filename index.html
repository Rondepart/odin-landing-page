I LOVE BANANA
Bananas are sweet, nutritious, and energy-boosting, packed with vitamins and potassium—perfect for a quick, healthy snack!
Different types of Banana
Cavendish bananas are sweet, creamy, and mildly fragrant, with smooth yellow skin and soft, pale flesh
Gros Michel banana is a sweet, rich, and creamy variety with a thick peel and intense banana flavor.
Bluggoe banana is a starchy, firm variety often used cooked rather than raw.
Dognana is a rare and quirky creature—a dog appearing after peeling a banana, spawns due to black magic. Pet, do not consume.
